iOS:
  1. Open the Settings app on your phone.
  2. Go to Mobile Service > SIMs.
  3. Click Add eSIM.
  4. Select Use QR code.
  5. Scan the eSIM QR code that you received from your network provider.
  6. Follow the instructions on your device to complete the installation.
Android:
  1. Open the Settings app on your phone.
  2. Select Network & Internet.
  3. Tap SIMs, and then Download eSIM.
  4. Select Next and scan the QR code you received from your network carrier.
  5. Tap Activate to start using your new Lyca number.
